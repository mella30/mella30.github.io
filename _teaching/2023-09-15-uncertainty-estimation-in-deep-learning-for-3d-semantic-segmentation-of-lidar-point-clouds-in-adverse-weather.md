Supervised a student project (master's thesis) on uncertainty estimation for LiDAR-based 3D semantic segmentation in adverse weather conditions.

Main focus areas:

- deep ensembles for uncertainty estimation and calibration
- robustness of semantic segmentation under fog, snow, and related domain shifts
- evaluation on SemanticKITTI and SemanticSTF
- analysis of confidence calibration and predictive reliability in adverse weather

The thesis investigated how uncertainty-aware models can improve both segmentation performance and robustness for autonomous driving perception.
