Supervised a student project (master's thesis) on separating and analyzing different uncertainty sources in deep learning for LiDAR perception.

Main focus areas:

- systematic study of aleatoric, epistemic, and label uncertainty
- synthetic dataset generation with CARLA for point-wise semantic segmentation
- simulation of out-of-distribution objects and different labeling noise processes
- analysis of how different uncertainty sources affect perception reliability

The thesis focused on building controlled datasets and evaluation setups that make uncertainty effects in autonomous driving perception more interpretable.
